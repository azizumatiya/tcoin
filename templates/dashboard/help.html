Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions about the crypto mining dashboard and learn how to maximize your earnings

Frequently Asked Questions

How does mining work?

Click the 'Start Mining' button on the dashboard. A timer will begin, and once it completes, you'll receive coins based on your current level and mining rate. You can see the progress bar fill up during the cycle.

What determines my mining rate?

Your mining rate is influenced by your level and any upgrades you purchase in the shop. Higher levels and better upgrades increase your mining rate, allowing you to earn more coins per cycle.

How do I level up?

You level up by earning experience points (EXP). EXP is gained through mining, claiming bonuses, and referring friends. Each level requires more EXP to reach the next level, and your mining rate improves as you level up.

What are bonuses?

Bonuses are additional rewards you can claim daily or earn through referrals. Daily bonuses give you a random amount of coins, while referral bonuses reward you with coins for each friend who joins using your referral link.

How do referrals work?

On the Referrals page, you'll find your unique referral link. Share it with friends, and when they sign up using your link, you'll earn a bonus. You can track your referred users and total bonus earned on the Referrals page.

Can I buy upgrades?

Yes, you can purchase upgrades in the Shop to improve your mining rate and earn more coins. Upgrades can be bought using the coins you've earned from mining, bonuses, or referrals.
